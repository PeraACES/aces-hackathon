What is ACES Hackathon?

Hackathon 2025, organized by the Association of Computer Engineering Students (ACES) of the University of Peradeniya, is one of Sri Lanka's premier idea-pitching competitions.
Under the theme "Digital Transformation," participants will develop tools, apps, and strategies addressing challenges across various fields. For the first time, the competition is open to all universities and includes a special category for school students, expanding its impact.
